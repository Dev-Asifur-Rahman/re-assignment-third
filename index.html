What are some popular house design styles I can consider?

Popular styles include modern, farmhouse, colonial, and eco-friendly. Each style offers unique features, from clean lines in modern homes to rustic charm in farmhouse designs.

How can I maximize my budget when building a house?

Set priorities, choose cost-effective materials, and consider an open floor plan. Hiring an experienced contractor can also help save on costs.

What should I consider when choosing a floor plan?

Think about your lifestyle, space needs, and future growth. Plan for the number of rooms and openness based on how you’ll use the space.
